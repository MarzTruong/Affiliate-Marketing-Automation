Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

1. Overview

Affiliate Auto Publisher ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

When you authorize this App via TikTok OAuth, we may collect:

  • Your TikTok User ID and display name (for account identification only)
  • OAuth access tokens (to publish content on your behalf)
  • Content you create within the App (scheduled posts, captions)

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account
  • Schedule and publish content to your TikTok account
  • Display your publishing history within the App

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally in a private database accessible only to you. We do not store data on third-party servers or share it with any external parties.

5. TikTok Data

Data obtained through the TikTok API is used solely for the purposes described in this policy and in accordance with TikTok's Platform Policy. We do not sell, rent, or share TikTok user data.

6. Data Retention

OAuth tokens are stored until you revoke access or uninstall the App. You can revoke access at any time via TikTok's app permission settings.

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

8. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by revoking the App's access in your TikTok account settings.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via GitHub: github.com/MarzTruong
